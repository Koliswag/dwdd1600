Our Mission

Here at Koli Clothing, our mission is to create and provide and inspire. We belive that fashion and style is one of the most valuable and uniqe forms of expression. We strive to provide the means of expression and exploration to all that venture to find personal and individual style. We embody various cultures of athleticism and social outlets producing quality gear for athletes, hobbyists, and explorers alike. We hope to inspire as we create and present what to us is more than just clothing.

Cultures

We support and create clothing based on styles found from 3 main athletic groups and the cultures that are associated with them. We draw inspiration not only from the many athletes that participate in these outlets but also from the history and background of these individual sports.

Skateboarding

Skateboarding, making its real cultural debut in the late 70's - early 80's, has deeply influenced the lives of innumerable children, teens, and adults accross the nation and world. The culture that has been built through the rising popularity of this sport is one that defines entire generations and has givin birth to entirely new categories of style and fashion. Here at Koli Clothing we embrace the skating culture, creating tough and reliable skate gear that both skaters and those who admire the streetwear can enjoy and embrace the culture through wearing.
